## â“ FAQ

### Q: Do users need to install?
**A:** No! Web app works in browser too. Install is optional but recommended.

### Q: Will it work on iOS?
**A:** Yes! Use "Add to Home Screen" (same as native PWA experience)

### Q: Can I update the app?
**A:** Yes! Service Worker auto-updates in background.

### Q: What about offline changes?
**A:** Changes are queued locally, auto-sync when online (optional).

### Q: Is it secure?
**A:** Yes! HTTPS required, authentication protected, validated cache.

### Q: How much storage used?
**A:** ~100-150 MB locally (auto-cleaned after 24 hours)

### Q: Works without internet?
**A:** Yes! View cached data, can't create new records offline.

### Q: What browsers support it?
**A:** Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox all supported!
